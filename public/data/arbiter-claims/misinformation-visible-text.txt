What this says about the discourse

The most “claim-like” misinformation content in this dataset isn’t mainly health or science—it’s about (a) geopolitical narratives, (b) government/platform control and moderation, and (c) high-salience breaking-news misinformation dynamics.
A large share of high-engagement posts use “misinformation” as a label or accusation without providing a checkable statement; the extractable claims come from a smaller subset where people assert specific actions, dates, policies, or events.
